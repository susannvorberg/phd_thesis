2.2.1 Sequence Reweighting

Every sequence \(x_n\) of length \(L\) in an alignment with \(N\) sequences has an associated weight \(w_n\):

\[\begin{equation} w_n = \frac{1}{\sum_{m=1}^N I \left( ID(x_n, x_m) \geq 0.8 \right)} \\ ID(x_n, x_m)=\frac{1}{L} \sum_{i=1}^L I(x_n^i = x_m^i) \tag{2.1} \end{equation}\]

The number of effective sequences Neff of an alignment is then computed as:

\[\begin{equation} \text{Neff} = \sum_{n=1}^N w_n \tag{2.2} \end{equation}\]
